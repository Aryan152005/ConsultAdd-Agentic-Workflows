- Required certifications and licenses: Proposals must include copies of any necessary licenses, certifications, registrations, or permits from relevant authorities.
- Background checks: Contractors must conduct background checks on all workers.
- Worker qualifications: Workers must be at least 18 years old, sober, reliable, clean, appropriately dressed, able to work in public areas, and willing to follow supervisors' instructions.
- Financial stability: Contractors must demonstrate financial stability, which can be shown through a W-9, Certificate of Insurance, or a CPA letter with a financial solvency statement for at least one year.
- Disqualifiers: Workers who fail to meet the specified qualifications will not be utilized or paid by MHMR.
